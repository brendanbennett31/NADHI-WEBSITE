Deep in the forest, the last of an ancient tribe tenuously holds onto its way of life amid disturbing omens and a brutal drought. As the pressure of development slowly encroaches into their village and his people become tempted by the promise of life in the outside world, Shankar — the newly entrusted leader of the tribe — fights for their survival and their sacred land.

A meditation on what is lost when a way of being slips beneath the tide of progress — and on the weight a single person carries when they are the last to remember.
